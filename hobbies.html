How to Ski

  1. Stand upright while keeping your balance.
  2. Keep knees slightly bent as you descend down the hill.
  3. Turn yourself by shifting your weight towards your downhill ski.
  4. Kepp yourself upright as your body starts to turn on the slope.
  5. Repeatly turn across the hill to control your speed and descent.
  6. Shortly before you reach the bottom of the hill, prepare to stop.
  7. Stop by turning your skiis inward creating a "pizza" for a beggineer stop
  8. Keep this form until you eventually stop moving.
  9. Congrats on making it down your first hill!
